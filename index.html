NAME

ediz — software engineer and mechanistic interpretability researcher

SYNOPSIS

ediz [--software-engineer] [--llm-interpretability] [--popper]

ediz --work
ediz --research
ediz --files
ediz --contact

DESCRIPTION

Ediz Ucar (b. 2002) is a software engineer based in the United Kingdom. He works at Entrust on hardware security modules (HSMs) and related cybersecurity infrastructure.

His technical interests include mechanistic interpretability of language models, sparse autoencoders, and Popperian approaches to epistemology.

WORK

Entrust — Software Engineer
Development and maintenance of systems involving HSMs, key management, and secure cryptographic operations.

RESEARCH

Ediz has worked on mechanistic interpretability of large language models, with a focus on sparse autoencoders and feature evolution across layers.

  • Evolution of SAE Features Across Layers in LLMs
    link: https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.08869

PHILOSOPHY

Ediz is broadly influenced by Popperian critical rationalism: conjectures, attempts at refutation, and preference for transparent mechanisms over opaque claims.
