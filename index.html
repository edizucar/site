DESCRIPTION

Ediz Ucar is a software engineer based in the United Kingdom. He works at Entrust on hardware security modules (HSMs) and related cybersecurity infrastructure.

His technical interests include mechanistic interpretability of language models, sparse autoencoders, and philosophy of science.
